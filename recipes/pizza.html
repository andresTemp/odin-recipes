Ingredients

  • 2 teaspoons yeast
  • 2 tablespoons warm water
  • 3 cup sheredded cheese, divided
  • 1 large egg, lightly beaten
  • 1 cup flour
  • 2 ounces sliced pepperoni

Steps

  1. Mix Yeast into warn water into a small cup until dissolved.
  2. Into a medium microwave-safe bowl stir yeast mixture and egg until combined.
  3. Stir in flour until well incorporated.
  4. Knead dough by hand for 2 minutes.
  5. Place dough onto baking sheet and press with your fingers into a thin crust about 11 or 12 inches in diameter.
  6. Bake in the oven until lightly browned.
  7. Spread pizza sauce over crust, Top with cup cheese and scatter pepperoni over top
  8. Return to the oven and bake until cheese is melted.
